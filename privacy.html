Privacy Policy for Urgency Bar

This page explains what information Urgency Bar collects, how it is used, and how AIssess protects it when the app is installed on a Shopify store.

Effective Date: 24 March 2026

Urgency Bar (“the App”) is operated by AIssess, a UK-based software provider. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you install and use the App with your Shopify store.

1. Information We Collect

When you install and use the App, we may collect:

  • Shopify store information (such as shop domain and store settings)
  • Product and inventory data required to display urgency messaging
  • App usage data (such as interactions with features and onboarding steps)

We do not collect or process personal customer data from your store.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • operate and display the urgency bar on your store
  • enable app functionality and configuration
  • provide internal analytics to improve app performance and reliability

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse.

4. Cookies and Tracking

The App may use cookies or similar technologies to support functionality and measure feature usage (for example, tracking whether the urgency bar has been displayed).

These are used solely to operate and improve the App.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the App, including:

  • Shopify APIs
  • Resend (for transactional email delivery)

These services process data only as necessary to provide their functionality.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to operate the App and provide its functionality.

7. Uninstall and Data Removal

If you uninstall the App, associated app data will no longer be processed and may be deleted from our systems.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
